Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What should I do if a book is lost?

If a book is lost, please inform the library administration management immediately. You will either need to replace the book or pay the required fine amount.

How can I get a library membership?

You can get a membership by visiting the main counter with your National Identity Card (NIC) and a document to verify your address, or you can register online through our website.

How many books can I borrow at one time?

A regular member can borrow a maximum of 3 books at a time for a period of 14 days.
